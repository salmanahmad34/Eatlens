Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is the nutrition analysis?
Our AI-powered analysis is highly accurate for common foods, with an average accuracy of 85-90%. For complex dishes with multiple ingredients, accuracy may vary. We continuously improve our algorithms with more data.
Can I use the free plan forever?
Yes! The free plan offers 3 image analyses per month indefinitely. If you need more analyses or advanced features, you can upgrade to our Pro plan at any time.
Is my data secure?
Absolutely. We use industry-standard encryption and never share your personal data or food images with third parties. Your privacy is our top priority.
What happens if I exceed my free analyses?
Once you've used your 3 free analyses for the month, you'll need to wait until the next month or upgrade to our Pro plan for unlimited analyses.
